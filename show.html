AR2 - Vuforia Studio Project

Vuforia Studio and Creo Illustrate

Our group was working for FieldLab, where, among other things, mechanical engineers work with robots and AR experiences. We used Vuforia Studio, Creo Illustrate and Vuforia View to complete our tasks. Our first task was to create javascript code for bindings, which act as connectors that link augmented reality (AR) experiences with real-world data sources and systems. Our second task was to create a sequence for a model in Creo Illustrate, then import it to Vuforia Studio and make it usable there in a way that lets the users choose the step in the animation that they would like to be played. The goal was to be able to watch the complete animation, or just steps of a sequence and skip between them. Our final task was to add the possiblity to highlight certain parts of the model and get information from an API, which would then be shown in a pop-up.
